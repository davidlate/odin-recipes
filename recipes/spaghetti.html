Spaghetti

This is what spaghetti looks like

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pasta sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. First, bring about 3 cups of water to a boil in a pot
  2. Break the spaghetti in half and place it in the water. All parts of the pasta should be completely submerged
  3. Wait 8 minutes. Then, use a colander to drain the water from the pasta
  4. Place the pasta back into the dry pot. Then, add garlic salt, pasta sauce, and a small amount of olive oil to taste and stir
