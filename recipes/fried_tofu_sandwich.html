Dave's Pan-Fried Tofu Sandwich

A tasty, indulgent alternative to the standard daily fare

Some days, we need a quick meal to satisfy our hunger, but the standard toast or peanut butter jelly just won't cut it.

This sandwich is a filling, refreshing, indulgent way to make up a calorie deficit quickly and enjoyably.

Ingredients

  • Sliced White Bread
  • Tofu
  • Earth Balance Butter
  • 1/3 of a tomato
  • Cooking or Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Slice the tofu along the horizontal plane to make 3-4 large rectangular slices
  2. Place a small amount of oil in a pan, then place 2 tofu slices in the pan
  3. Turn on the heat to high, being mindful to lower the heat if the oil starts to boil
  4. Move the tofu around so it doesn't stick, and flip when browned
  5. Slice the tomato
  6. Toast 4 slices of bread and apply Earth Balance butter
  7. Place the tofu in the bread and top with a tomato. CAREFUL! The tofu may still be very hot
