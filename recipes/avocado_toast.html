Avocado Toast

This is what avocado toast looks like

This is a great recipe for when you have avocados, and want to put them on toast

The crispiness of the avocado complements the crunchiness of the toast, and tastes great.

Ingredients

  • One avocado
  • 2 slices of white bread
  • Lemon juice
  • salt (sea salt is preferable)
  • pepper and tomato are optional

Steps

  1. Put the bread in the toaster
  2. While the bread is toasting:
    1. Wash the avocado
    2. Slice open the avocado with a dull knife or spoon
    3. Scoop the contents of the avocado into a bowl, and discard the seed
  3. Use a fork to mash the avocado contents to a paste. Add lemon juice, salt, and/or pepper to taste at this stage
  4. Spread the avocado contents onto the toast and enjoy!
